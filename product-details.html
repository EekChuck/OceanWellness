Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Ocean Executive different from other coaching programs?

Ocean Executive stands apart through its comprehensive approach that combines evidence-based coaching with done-for-you services. Unlike generic wellness programs, we customize every aspect of your protocol based on your unique biochemistry, lifestyle, and goals. Our team includes specialists in functional medicine, performance psychology, and behavioral science who collaborate to address the root causes of your health challenges, not just symptoms.

How much time will I need to commit each week?

We design the Ocean Executive program with busy professionals in mind. Beyond your weekly 60-minute coaching session, implementation typically requires 2-3 hours per week, which includes your personalized movement practice, stress management routines, and meal preparation (though much of this can be outsourced if desired). Our done-for-you elements are specifically crafted to minimize your time investment while maximizing results.

What results can I expect and how quickly?

Most Ocean Executive clients report noticeable improvements in energy levels and mental clarity within the first 2-3 weeks. More significant results typically emerge within 90 days, including improved sleep quality, enhanced stress resilience, sustainable weight management, and optimized biomarkers. Long-term benefits include improved longevity markers, sustained high performance, and the establishment of habits that support lifelong wellness.

Can the program accommodate my travel schedule?

Absolutely. The Ocean Executive program is specifically designed for professionals who travel frequently. Your coach will develop custom travel protocols including hotel workout plans, restaurant ordering guides for different cuisines, jet lag mitigation strategies, and sleep optimization techniques for different time zones. Coaching sessions can be conducted virtually from anywhere in the world, and your coach will adjust your protocol based on your changing schedule and environment.

Is there a minimum commitment period?

We recommend a minimum three-month commitment to the Ocean Executive program to experience meaningful, sustainable results. Lasting transformation takes time, and our approach is designed to create long-term change rather than quick fixes. Many of our clients choose to continue with the program long-term, transitioning to a maintenance phase after achieving their initial goals.
